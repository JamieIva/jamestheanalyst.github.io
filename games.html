Video Game Sales

In this project we take a look at the sales of video game consoles from respectable companies. The companies we will be looking at are Sony’s PlayStation and Microsoft’s Xbox. Due to the competitiveness between these two companies I would like to see which company has sold more units and what genre of video games is their best seller.

QUESTIONS TO ANSWER

  1. What is the best-selling genre for each console?
  2. Which company sells the most games?
  3. In what year did both consoles sell the most units?
  4. What year did video games have the most sales?
  5. Which console is closing the gap behind both PlayStation and XBOX?

Hypothesis

  1. Action games are the selling ticket for both consoles. Each year action games are released under different subgenres, for example, battle royal games.
  2. PlayStation sells the most games across all platforms and have been dominating the market for years.
  3. Xbox had its best-selling year in 2011 when they released their famously known Xbox 360. After 2011 their sales started to plateau.

    Sony’s PlayStation’s had its best-selling year in 2013. That year they released their new product, the PlayStation 4 and a number of video game exclusives which made it the best-selling console behind the PlayStation 2.
  4. 2008 was the year video games sold the most games.
  5. Nintendo is the video company closing its gap to its competitors.
