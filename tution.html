University Tuition

In this project we take a look at the price of tuition in the United States from 2014 to 2021.

QUESTIONS TO ANSWER

  1. Has the price of fees increase through the years and which years had the most uptick?
  2. Tuition comparison between public schools and private schools?
  3. Which form of service or type of university will be better?

Hypothesis

  1. Yes, the price of tuition has gone over the years and 2020 was the year where the prices for tuition went up.
  2. Private universities are more expensive.
  3. Private Universities exposes the children to possibilities that public universities don’t have access to, such as; better gyms, well-funded sports programs, better learning facilities which would be detrimental to further their growth, because of being funneled through private universities puts them at an advantage.
